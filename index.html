What is a Third Culture Kid?

A third culture kid, or TCK for short,
is an individual who is raised in a culture
different from their parents or nationality.
Sometimes this can create an identity crisis for
these indivudals as they cannot associate themselves
with a culture confidently.
However, they do have strengths such as the ability
to assimilate with other cultures.
