About

Who are we?

We are a church ministry dedicated to third culture
kids (TCKS) of Seoul Immanuel Church. Through
the hard work of administration, the ministry has grown
and extended its reach to other churches and people
worldwide

What do we do?

We provide worship services for third culture kids and
connect them with experts in the job field
in order to help them with creating their own identity
and future in the name of God.
